About BetterScent

Why BetterScent?

I created BetterScent for my Insight Data Science project. It was born out of a problem I had a few months back - I was looking for perfume that was similar in scent to the perfume I already had. I went to a major perfume department store and asked a salesperson if she could recommend a new fragrance for me, given the one I already use. She was helpful, but there were a few issues with her recommendations that I realized that no person could not solve easily.

First, there was no way she could have smelled all 200+ perfumes and colognes in the store. Not only is the process time-consuming, but it would essentially be impossible for anyone to memorize every single fragrance and be able to retrieve details about each fragrance upon customer request.

Second, odor fatigue is a real thing. We've all had that problem where after smelling three different fragrances, everything after starts smelling the same. This problem also means that if you choose to purchase the tenth one you smell, it's not going to smell the same when you go home and try it on again.

To solve these issues, I created BetterScent - a perfume recommender that "smells" them all for you. You tell BetterScent the fragrance you currently use, it will find similar perfumes and recommend one with high user ratings - one that smells similar to whatever you already have.

How does it work?

1. Tell us the fragrance you currently use.

2. We will find similar fragrances using our large database of over 42,000 fragrances.

3. Of those, we will recommend a fragrance with the highest user ratings.
